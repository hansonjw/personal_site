## Space & Exploration

What an age we live in -- new horizons, exploration, engineering, technology and venturing further into space.

## Synchronicity

The three eclipse elements are deliberately primary: blue, red, yellow. This is a conscious nod to the artwork of The Police's Synchronicity album — bold, elemental, and unmistakable.

## Physics

There is something about primary colors used with restraint.  They don't compete with each other. Each one holds its own space.  Anthey overlay each in a strange way.  A nod to physics with Isaac Newton and his prism, as well as Roger Penrose and his triangle.

## Compound Interest

Financial wellbeing and education is a passion of mine.  The subtle arc within the main circle traces an upward curve from lower-left to upper-right. This is a visual metaphor for compound interest.  The idea is simple: small, consistent inputs accumulate into something extraordinary over time. The arc is quiet, almost hidden within the circle. But it is always there.

## A Collaboration with AI

The logo itself was a fun experiment in human-AI collaboration. The concept, the colors, the symbolism — all of that came from a conversation. The geometry was built iteratively, nudging an AI to translate ideas into SVG code, one tweak at a time.

It's a fitting origin for a site about technology and curiosity. The tool was new, the process was exploratory, and the result was something neither party could have arrived at alone.
